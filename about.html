About the Foundation

Shreemad Jagadguru Sai Baba Pratishthan was established on 11th November 1993 with key goals:

  • Supporting neglected and underprivileged sections of society
  • Promoting cultural values and traditions

Sai Sanskar Sanstha was founded on 1st July 1996 to start a special school for intellectually disabled children.

The foundation stone was laid by Governor Dr. P. C. Alexander.

The small beginning has now grown into a grand 3-story building with workshops and training for youth.
